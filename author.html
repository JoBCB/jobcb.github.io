[ Choreo-Work }

Bringing choreographic + programming + graphic languages into one new communication form. Bringing choreographic thinking into interface design — developing new ways of navigating through both analogue and digital environments.

Joana Chicau [PT] is a media designer and performer, with a background in classical ballet and contemporary dance.
In her practice she investigates the possible connections between – and coexistence of – choreography and communication/ media design, aiming to open the possibilities for new aesthetic, energetic and social dimensions.
